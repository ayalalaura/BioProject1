Laura Ayala is a Marketing Associate with half a decade of experience in NYC non-profits and museums. Most recently the Community Engagement Associate
at the internationally recognized Bronx Museum of the Arts, Laura is a community engagement pro adept at building meaningful relationships, engaging visitors
across a wide demographic, and learning new technologies on the fly.

Off the clock, the lifelong Bronxite enjoys indulging in all things geek culture, reading graphic novels ("Paper Girls" and "Watchmen" are a couple of her faves),
knitting cuddly creatures, and practicing vinyasa yoga. If she had a superpower, it would be telekinesis.
