MORRIS PARK, BRONX, NY


In the east Bronx neighborhood of Morris Park, the locals know each other by name. Many stop to exchange more than a quick hello and
engage in more than small talk. It’s the kind of neighborhood that some nostalgically speak of when they bring up their childhood in
New York City: the good old days when an area was a neighborhood. But the thing about Morris Park is that it still is a neighborhood
